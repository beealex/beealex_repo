About Me

My name is Bianka Alexander and I am currently a senior in the Digital Media, Arts, and Technology Program at Penn State Behrend. I am focusing on the Digital Humanites and Sound and Motion categories within the DIGIT Program, and have also completed two minors; Mass Media and Communication and Media Production. Additionally, I will be staying one more year at Behrend to pursue minors in Management Information Systems and Data Visualization.

In my time outside of class, I work in the Galley/Bruno's at Penn State Behrend and am also an alumni of Theta Phi Alpha. Through these two positions I have gained a supervisor role in the Galley and have also held two chair positions, Secretary and Risk Management, in Theta Phi Alpha.
